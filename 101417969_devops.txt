George Brown College is a public college focused on applied arts and technology with three different campuses located in downtown Toronto.
It was opened in 1967 by the government of Ontario, meaning it has been apart of Toronto for several decades.
It offers more than 170 full-time programs in art and design, business, community services, early childhood education, and much more, giving students a variety of subjects and courses to take.
The college offers several different diploma, advanced diploma, and degree programs for its various courses, allowing students to choose the level of education they desire in their chosen field.
