George Brown College has the course COMP3104 - DevOps as part of its T177 Program.
In this class, students are taught about the procedures and protocols that DevOps engineers use in the workplace to increase the productivity of the company.
They are tasked with analyzing and evaluating multiple developer operation implementations and implement hands-on work using industry standard toolkits.
Students will use many websites and different pieces of software throughout this course, including Github, Gitbash, Visual Studio Code, and more.
