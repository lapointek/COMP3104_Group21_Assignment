Importance of DevOps: 
The importance of DevOps is automation.It is a set of practices that improve software delivery speed. 
DevOps improves collaboration and communication, increases agility, cost savings, improves quality and reduces defects. 
DevOps also allows teams to work better with business users who can provide valuable feedback early on in the process.

Benefits of DevOps:
Speed - Innovate for customers faster 
Rapid Delivery - Increase frequency and pace of releases 
Reliability - Ensure the quality of application updates and infrastructure changes
Improved collaboration - Build more effective teams under a DevOps cultural model
Security - Move quickly while retaining control and preserving compliance
